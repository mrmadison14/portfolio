What's real vs. simulated: Sanitized rebuild of a production on-call paging service (live version: serverless functions, a queue, a scheduler, and an SMS provider). This simulator runs the real state machine in your browser: trigger an alert, watch the page fire, then acknowledge it or let the 15-minute timer lapse to see escalation. Meridian Fleet is fictional; no SMS is sent.
